Rules

  • The game is divide into three stages.
  • To go to the next stage,touch the gate and solve the puzzle.
  • You have three lives to complete the game.
  • If you touch an obstacle,you lose a heart and go back to the starting point.
  • You have two puzzle points.If you write wrong answer to a puzzle you lose a puzzle point and move to the next stage. You can also use a puzzle point to skip a puzzle.
